Drumpet

Drumpet began in Mexico City in 2017. It is a project of two musicians graduated from the Ukrainian Conservatory.

The project is integrated by Alex into drums, electric bass, keyboard and percussion. Yuliya on the trumpet, sax and keyboards.

Alex and Yuliya have worked in different countries such as: China, Turkey, Germany, Ukraine, Russia and the Arab Emirates in different projects and performing different musical genres.

  • Drumpet plays different musical styles such as electronica, electro pop and Lounge.
  • Drumpet has a unique show with a style that separates it from other projects.
  • Drumpet is a musical experience that you have to experience.

Drumpet comenzó en la Ciudad de México en 2017. Es un proyecto de dos músicos graduados del conservatorio de Ucrania.

El proyecto está integrado por Alex en la batería, bajo eléctrico, teclado y percusiones. Yuliya en la trompeta, sax y teclados.

Alex y Yuliya han trabajado en diferentes países como: China, Turquía, Alemania, Ucrania, Rusia y Emiratos árabes en diferentes proyectos e interpretando diversos géneros musicales.

  • Drumpet toca diferentes estilos musicales como electrónica, electro pop y Lounge.
